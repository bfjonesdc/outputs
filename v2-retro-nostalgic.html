Meet Char.

A Long Beach native turned D.C. resident, Char is the bakerista behind the jar. She's as daring in the kitchen as she is in life — pairing chocolate chips with Oreos before it was cool, and slipping real Reese's cups into cookies because, well, why not?

Her cookies are baked, packed, and shipped with love. Carefully crafted recipes, wholesome ingredients, homemade taste. The perfect gift for co-workers, friends, family — or a treat to keep all to yourself.

The Fine Print

How long do orders take?
All orders take 48 to 72 hours to be baked and packed. We bake fresh — nothing sits on a shelf.
Do you ship nationwide?
Yes — we ship across the U.S. Local D.C.-area pickup is also available; just choose pickup at checkout.
Do you have vegan options?
We sure do. Three vegan flavors: Chocolate Chip, Chocolate Chip and Oreo, and Chocolate Chip with Nuts. Same crave-worthy taste, plant-based.
Can I order custom or special-occasion cookies?
Absolutely. Reach out via the contact form below or email charcookiejar@gmail.com to talk through your event, wedding, or corporate order.
What about allergens?
Our kitchen handles wheat, dairy, eggs, nuts, peanuts, and soy. We can't guarantee an allergen-free environment, so please order with care.
